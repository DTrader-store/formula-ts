# Simple Moving Average (MA) Example
# Calculates 5-day, 10-day, and 20-day moving averages

# Short-term MA (5 days)
MA5:MA(CLOSE,5);

# Medium-term MA (10 days)
MA10:MA(CLOSE,10);

# Long-term MA (20 days)
MA20:MA(CLOSE,20);

# Golden Cross Signal: MA5 crosses above MA10
GOLDEN_CROSS:CROSS(MA5,MA10);

# Death Cross Signal: MA10 crosses above MA20
DEATH_CROSS:CROSS(MA10,MA20);
